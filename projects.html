This project is a basketball simulator. You can pick any player that is a starter in the NBA. You have a choice where you can either shoot a 3 pointer, layup, mid-range, or a dunk. Your chances of making these depends on how good the player that you chose is. You are playing against a random opponent and you are trying to beat them to 30 points. Your goal is to get to the NBA Finals which happens after 5 games.

My second project is a CRUD app. With this Crud app you can create a roster for any sports team. You can view each player in your update a player, delete a player, create a new player, and view an individual player.
