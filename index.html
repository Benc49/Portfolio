Basketball Simulator

NBA Arcade Game: I developed this game with a classmate, using Python. You choose a player from a list of NBA teams and how you would like that player to attempt to score (layup, dunk, 3 point shot or mid-range shot). The players have their own set of attributes for each shot. The better the player you choose the higher the chance of success of scoring points on each shot. You are playing against the computer that is choosing random shots. There are four regular rounds and the winner goes on to the final round.
